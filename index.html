What Helm does for owners

Every feature is built around a simple question: what should the owner know right now, and what should they be able to do about it in one click?

Managers

Oversee every manager from one screen

Assign ownership of kitchen, bar, and front-of-house. Every metric that matters to their role rolls up to you in real time — food cost, bar pour cost, labor ratio, guest satisfaction, no-shows. When something's off, you see it before they do.

  • Per-manager scorecards with target vs. actual
  • Automatic alerts on drift: waste, overtime, voids, comps
  • 1:1 notes, reviews, and goals tracked alongside metrics

Par values, counts, and waste — without the clipboards

Set par values by item, location, and day of week. Managers count on their phone, Helm reconciles against your POS sales, and waste gets logged with a photo in five seconds. You see shrinkage trends across your whole operation in a single chart.

  • Theoretical vs. actual usage, updated nightly
  • Smart reorder suggestions by vendor, cutoff-aware
  • Waste by station, by shift, by manager on duty

Scheduling

Schedules that start with your actual forecast

Helm builds each week's draft from your forecasted covers and labor target, not last week's copy-paste. Your managers edit inside their own view; you approve from yours. Staff see shifts and swap in the app.

  • Labor-ratio guardrails per shift
  • Certifications, break law, and minor rules enforced
  • No-show and late-arrival history surfaced on the schedule

Cashflow

A cashflow sheet that forecasts itself

Connect your bank in two clicks. Helm projects your next 30, 60, and 90 days from your real deposit patterns, your upcoming bills, your payroll runs, and your seasonal history. Owners who've never modeled cashflow in their lives suddenly can.

  • Plaid bank sync — balances and transactions update hourly
  • Projected low-water mark with drill-down to the cause
  • "What if I push the rent 3 days" scenarios, one toggle

Bills

Every bill lands in one inbox

Forward vendor bills — or have them sent directly — to bills@yours.helm.app. Helm extracts the line items, matches them to your vendors and GL codes, and drops the total straight onto your cashflow forecast. Approve pay dates with one click.

  • PDF, image, and EDI invoice parsing
  • Duplicate detection across vendors and locations
  • Bank-synced payment status — no more "did this get paid?"
